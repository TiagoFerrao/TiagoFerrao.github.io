What is this & when to use it ▾

An Opportunity Solution Tree (Teresa Torres) is a living map of discovery work. You set one measurable outcome, map the customer opportunities — needs, pains and desires heard in research — that drive it, compare competing solutions for the most promising opportunity, and de-risk them with small experiments. Unlike a one-off canvas, the tree lives: scores shift, evidence accumulates, branches get parked or killed, and the ★ target path always shows where the focus is.

Use it when

You have (or can define) a measurable outcome and need to decide what to build next; you keep jumping between ideas; or stakeholders push pet solutions and you need to show the reasoning between the goal and the backlog.

Don't use it when

You don't have an outcome yet — start with the Value Proposition Designer to understand jobs, pains and gains first — or decisions are already locked and you're in pure delivery mode.

How it fits the toolbox

Validated pains and jobs from the VPC become opportunities here; every experiment can be queued to the Assumptions Tracker to manage validation cost and remaining risk.
