// the process

An innovation process you can walk through

Every idea travels the same path: start from the story you want to tell the world, validate that someone actually has the problem, design the concept, stress-test it technically and financially, consolidate everything into a single business case, and plan how to validate the riskiest assumptions before the pitch.

Click any stage above to see what happens there — and watch the assumptions table follow you. It never leaves the process.
