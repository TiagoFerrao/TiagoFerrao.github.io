Education & Training

I tried to acquire knowledge in 3 areas I consider fundamental: Engineering (how things work), Economics (how the world works) and Management (how corporations work). Nowadays I focus more on digital skills.

Aveiro University

Environmental Engineering · 1995 - 2002 · Aveiro

This degree took 5 years to complete but it was totally worth it. 3 years spent on hardcore engineering (physics, calculus, chemistry, biology, zoology) and another 2 studying how pollutants and waste travel through ecosystems.

Wageningen University

Erasmus | Soil and Plant Analysis · 1999 - 2000 · Wageningen

During my ERASMUS period I did a short course on plant and soil analysis and travelled around central Europe with almost no money. A formative experience in independence and cultural exposure.

York University

Energy, Environment and Society · 2001 - 2002 · Toronto

I was lucky to study in Toronto for a short period. I arrived a couple of weeks before the 9/11 attacks in New York. The experience of being abroad during such a pivotal moment shaped my worldview significantly.

ISEG

Economics and Politics (Energy & Environment) · 2008 - 2009 · Lisbon

After a lot of technical work I got the feeling many decisions happen in a universe I could not quite understand, so I decided to take a master in economics. I knew nothing about monetary policy, inflation or markets — it opened a completely new perspective.

INDEG | ISCTE

Executive Master: Innovation Management · 2015 - 2016 · Lisbon

When two of my employers went bankrupt due to inability to cope with a turbulent and quick-changing context, I decided to take a management course that could also teach me about keeping up with the pace of change through innovation.

ISCTE

Master in Business Management · 2017 - 2018 · Lisbon

I enrolled in Business Management to have my thesis in innovation policy published and graded by an official management institution. A few months after starting, I resigned from my job and decided to pivot towards digital skills.

Le Wagon

Fullstack Development Bootcamp · 2020 · Lisbon

It was a long time since I had my brain challenged like this and I loved it. In 9 weeks I went from basic HTML knowledge to building full web applications with Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, and deploying to production.

Team Treehouse

Several Digital Skills · 2018 - present · the WWW

Team Treehouse is my go-to coding learning tool. From Photoshop to data science, this site has almost every digital skill one needs to learn in 3-minute videos and a good gamification engine.
