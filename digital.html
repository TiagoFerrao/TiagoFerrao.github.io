EnergyOFF

Energy Management Software · 2014 - 2019 · Lisbon

EnergyOFF was an original design to support energy managers of non-industrial facilities — logging consumption, generating integrated reports, and suggesting efficiency measures. As product owner I wrote the feature specs and procurement documents for V2, and the platform became the backbone of two national efficiency programmes: Freguesias + Eficientes (local parishes, with ANAFRE, RNAE and ERSE) and PIEE IPSS (social-sector institutions, with CNIS). › try this legacy lighting identification script

BioAtlas was the first Portuguese GIS for biomass resources in Portugal. I led this project while Energy Programme Manager at INTELI together with Aveiro University, AREANATEJO, ICNF and CBE. As my first digital project it represents one of the most relevant milestones of my digital career.
