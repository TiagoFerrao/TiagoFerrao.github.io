Digital Work

Projects and products from my digital career — from designing energy software features to full-stack development.

I started by creating technical software for the energy sector and slowly built up knowledge and tools, from UI/UX, basic HTML and lately some backend. Now I am playing with design and frontend, moving up the ladder of fullstack development until I am able to develop and integrate my own digital tools.

SportSpots

Sports Events Manager · 2018 - present · Lisbon

SportSpots is a digital platform to manage sport activities. I built this using methods and tools developed during my research thesis in digital platform development. Currently looking for investment to bring it to market.

EnergyOFF

Energy Management Software · 2014 - 2019 · Lisbon

EnergyOFF was an original design developed to support energy managers of non-industrial facilities. It logs energy consumptions, provides integrated reports and has a bit of AI that suggests efficiency measures.

BioAtlas

Biomass GIS Platform · 2013 - 2017 · Lisbon

BioAtlas was the first Portuguese GIS for biomass resources in Portugal. I led this project while Energy Programme Manager at INTELI together with Aveiro University, AREANATEJO, ICNF and CBE. As my first digital project it represents one of the most relevant milestones of my digital career.
